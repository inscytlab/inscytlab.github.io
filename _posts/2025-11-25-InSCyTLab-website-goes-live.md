InSCyTLab is the first self-formed IT research group in the Faculty of Science and Engineering at Southern Cross University. The founding members include Professor Haifeng Shen, Dr Ruitao Feng, Dr Lin Li, and Dr Alex Hendry who share a common interest in research innovation for improving and securing software development and systems through innovating artificial intelligence technologies. Our team aspires to conduct world-class research by publishing at premiere venues including international conferences and journals ranked A* by ICORE or A by CCF and journals ranked as top 10% in their fields. We are also keen to work closely with governments, industries, and communities to solve their problems through technological innovation.
