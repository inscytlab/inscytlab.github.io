Dr Lin Li is a lecturer at the Faculty of Science and Engineering, Southern Cross University. He is interested in a wide range of topics in Cybersecurity, Biometrics, Health care, and AI. His research is primarily focused on cybersecurity, with a particular emphasis on Physiological Signal-Based Biometrics, such as leveraging PPG-based (Photoplethysmography) methods, to enhance user authentication in various contexts. He also delves into security analysis and vulnerability detection within software systems, employing advanced AI-driven methods to identify and mitigate potential threats. Currently, he is particularly focused on the intersection of LLM and cybersecurity, aiming to create robust, security solutions that ensure both the efficacy and reliability of applications in real-world scenarios. His research has been recognized at top-tier conferences and journals in the fields of Computer Security and Machine Learning.
