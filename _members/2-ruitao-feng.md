Dr Ruitao Feng is a Lecturer in Software Engineering and Cybersecurity at Southern Cross University, Australia. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. His research focuses on software security, trustworthy AI, and collaborative security analytics, with applications in vulnerability detection, malware analysis, and anomaly detection. Dr Feng has published in top-tier venues such as ICSE, FSE, ASE, CCS, NDSS, and leading journals including IEEE TSE, TOSEM, TDSC, TIFS, and TSC. His work has been recognized with awards such as the ACM CCS Distinguished Artifact Award.
